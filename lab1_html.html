Sultan Azlan Shah Gallery

The Sultan Azlan Shah Gallery was inaugurated by His Majesty the Sultan of Perak, Sultan Azlan Muhibbuddin Shah on 9 December 2003. This magnificent gallery was restored from the old palace, Ulu Palace, which was first built in 1898 and completed in 1903.
