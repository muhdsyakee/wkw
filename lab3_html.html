About

Stephen Curry is an American professional basketball player who represents the ‘Golden State Warriors’ in ‘NBA.’ The first child of Dell Curry, who is considered one of the best 3-point shooters in ‘NBA’ history, Stephen has been named the greatest shooter by many players as well as analysts.

Stephen Curry is an ESTP personality type. He loves being in the spotlight and he knows how to charm. Not one to sit around contemplating philosophical topics, Stephen Curry prefers to be out in the world taking action.
